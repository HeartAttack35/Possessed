# Product Overview

**Possessed** is a horror visual novel game built with Ren'Py. It's a narrative-driven interactive story with branching choices and multiple endings.

## Genre & Theme
- Horror/Psychological thriller
- Multiple character perspectives
- Choice-driven narrative with affinity and mental state tracking
- Contains mature content: violence, gore, strong language

## Story
A group of friends explores an abandoned orphanato (orphanage) rumored to be a former torture center. The story involves supernatural elements, chase sequences, and survival horror mechanics.

## Key Features
- Multiple protagonists (Rodrigo, Luz, Azura, Cutipye, Nagi)
- Affinity system tracking relationships with characters
- Mental state system affecting gameplay
- Multiple endings (good, neutral, bad variations)
- Chase/escape sequences with time pressure
- Psychological distortion effects
- Chapter-based progression with save checkpoints
- Full audio design (music, SFX, ambient)

## Target Experience
Immersive horror narrative where player choices impact character relationships, survival, and endings. Atmosphere created through visual effects (distortion, filters), sound design, and tension-building mechanics.
